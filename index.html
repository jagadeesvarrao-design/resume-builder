Frequently Asked Questions

What is an ATS-friendly resume?
An ATS (Applicant Tracking System) friendly resume is specifically formatted to be easily read by the automated software employers use to screen candidates. It avoids complex tables, graphics, and unreadable fonts that cause tracking systems to reject your application automatically. Our builder creates pure vector-text PDFs that parse perfectly every time.
Is ZenResume really free to use?
Yes, ZenResume is completely free. Unlike other platforms that ask for a credit card right when you hit the download button, we provide high-quality PDF downloads at no cost. We are supported by optional advertisements and affiliate links.
Is my personal data secure?
Absolutely. All resume processing and AI importing happens securely. We do not sell your personal resume data to third-party recruiters. You have full control over your data and can delete your account at any time.
Can I import my existing resume?
Yes! Using our Gemini AI Magic Import feature, you can upload any messy PDF resume or copy-paste a text document. Our AI will automatically extract your experience, education, and skills and place them perfectly into our templates.
