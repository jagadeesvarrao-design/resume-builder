About Us

Empowering job seekers to build high-quality, professional resumes—100% free and private.

ZenResume was created in 2026 with a simple mission: to build a premium, fast, and completely free alternative to expensive subscription-locked resume editors. Most builders lure users in with "free templates" only to place their download button behind a monthly billing gateway. We believe that job seekers should have access to professional tools without financial barriers.

Our Core Philosophy

  • 100% Free & No Signups: We do not collect your email address, push monthly subscriptions, or charge for premium template styling. The application is completely open and free to use.
  • Privacy First: Your resume data belongs to you. ZenResume has no backend databases; all your entered values are stored strictly in your browser's local cache (localStorage). No files are uploaded to servers, ensuring complete client-side data safety.
  • ATS-Compliant Structure: Our templates are designed strictly to pass automated Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS). They use standard, clean typography, single-column frameworks, and valid HTML/CSS that parser spiders can crawl effortlessly.

Who We Are

We are a small team of software developers and recruiting experts dedicated to demystifying the job application process. By combining recruiter-approved styling formats with modern web performance practices (including integration with Gemini AI for Magic Imports), we hope to save you time and help you secure interviews.
