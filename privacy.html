Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 12, 2026

At ZenResume, accessible from https://resume-builder-swart-sigma-93.vercel.app, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document outline how we handle and protect your information, including standard cookie disclosures required for third-party advertising partners like Google AdSense.

1. 100% Client-Side Storage & Data Privacy

Unlike traditional online resume builders, ZenResume operates entirely client-side. We **do not** run any backend databases or store your personal resume inputs on remote servers. All your entered details (contact details, work experience, projects, education) are stored locally in your browser's private cache (localStorage). You are in complete control of your data; you can clear your browser storage or export your workspace data as a local backup file (.json) to restore later.

2. Cookies and Web Beacons

ZenResume uses standard cookies to save user settings (like Light/Dark theme preferences) and to track overall traffic analytics. In addition, third-party services we use may place cookies on your browser:

  • Google AdSense: Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve personalized ads on our site based on your prior visits to our site and other pages on the internet.
  • Vercel Speed Insights & Web Analytics: We use Vercel's lightweight tracking to measure loading speeds and user experience scores (without collecting personal identifiers).
  • Google Analytics: We track anonymous traffic spikes and country locations to gauge tool utility.

3. Google AdSense & DoubleClick DART Cookies

Google is one of the third-party vendors on our site. Google uses cookies, known as DART cookies, to serve ads to our site visitors based upon their visit to ZenResume and other sites on the internet. However, visitors may choose to decline or control the use of DART cookies by visiting the Google Ad and Content Network Privacy Policy at the following URL: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads.

You can also opt-out of personalized interest-based advertising entirely by visiting the Google Ads Settings page here: https://adssettings.google.com/ or by visiting www.aboutads.info.

4. Third-Party Privacy Policies

ZenResume's Privacy Policy does not apply to other advertisers or websites. We advise you to consult the respective Privacy Policies of these third-party ad servers (such as Google AdSense) for more detailed information. It may include their practices and instructions about how to opt-out of certain options.

You can choose to disable cookies through your individual browser options. To know more detailed information about cookie management with specific web browsers, it can be found at the browsers' respective websites.

5. Consent

By using our website, you hereby consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its Terms and Conditions.
